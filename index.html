About Me

I was born in South India, and finished my computer Science engineering from Andhra University. After that I joined TCS as a junior software developer with mainframe as my main technology. Later I was sent to US to client location, where I quit TCS to join MBA at University of Florida. At University of Florida I was able to complete my MBA with highest degree honors with concentration in IT strategy.

For the last 5 years, I have been working as Sr. Java Developer for Energy Trading company in Greensburg, PA. I am happily married and I have a 3 year old son. I love to watch animation movies, enjoy restaurants and playing with my son.
